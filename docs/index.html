What it does

Two features in v1.0.0 — a landed-cost calculator that follows you across eBay, and auction-ending alerts.

🧮

Estimated total

A dashed-purple box beside the price shows item + tax + shipping on listing, search, watchlist, bids/offers, and home pages.

🟢🟡🔴

Shipping dots

A colored dot flags shipping at a glance — free, high on one count, high on both, or 📍🏃 pickup-only — against your own thresholds.

🔨

Bid calculator

On auctions and Best Offer listings, type a prospective bid (or an = formula) and see the live landed total — no eval().

⏰

Auction alerts

Get a sound + system notification as an auction crosses your "seconds left" thresholds. Click the alert to jump straight to the tab.

📦

Per-item breakdown

Expand any box for a live @ $X.XX/unit figure — divide a lot's total by a count, formulas included, for accurate per-piece pricing.

🌍

Ranges & currencies

Multi-variation price ranges and non-US-currency listings both compute correctly from the US figures eBay shows.

Install it

Loads unpacked in under a minute — no store listing needed.

  1. Download or clone the repo from GitHub.
  2. Open chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode (top-right).
  3. Click Load unpacked and select the project folder.
  4. Visit any eBay listing, search, or bids/offers page — the boxes appear automatically.

Tune the tax rate, shipping-flag thresholds, per-page toggles, and auction alerts from the toolbar popup. Every field takes effect on open eBay tabs live.
